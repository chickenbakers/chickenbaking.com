## Apps

### Random Acts of Art

*Curated art that never repeats* &middot; iPhone &middot; Free

A quiet, ad-free pocket museum for your phone. Open it and a beautiful public-domain artwork from The Metropolitan Museum of Art's open collection is already on screen — no spinner, no account. Swipe for another, in an order that never repeats. Pinch to look closer; tap the heart to keep what moves you.

The Met has put more than 200,000 works online and almost nobody sees them. Random Acts of Art is a way to meet that other 99 percent, one piece at a time. No ads, no accounts, no tracking — just you and the art.

[View on the App Store &rarr;](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/random-acts-of-art/id6762949606)
